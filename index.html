One of my personal pet peeves: business advice that leaves out all the nitty-gritty details.

Let’s say you want to find a business idea, maybe you’re wondering…

  • "How do I know if an idea is good or not?"
  • "What if my idea has already been done?"
  • "What if my idea hasn't been done — is that better or worse?"
  • "What if I'm not an expert?"

And the advice you get says something like, “Come up with a good idea”.

It's the business equivalent of this:

Many people who genuinely want to start a business struggle simply because they don't have a clue what to do next. Most likely, they're using the wrong system. Or worse, no system at all.

(Systems are so important that they’re the bedrock of what I taught the folks on my Netflix show “How to Get Rich” to get out of debt, make money, and live their Rich Lives.)

It's like trying to bake a cake without a recipe or all the ingredients. Maybe an expert baker could make it work, but a beginner can't.

That's why one of the core principles behind my Earnable Live program is SHOWING, not telling.

Instead of just telling you to find a business idea — or giving you a list of 20 generic ones — Earnable Live…

  • Includes hours of Q&A and live business idea makeovers so you can SEE business ideas being developed and refined right before your eyes

And of course, Earnable Live also lets you get rapid feedback on your ideas from the community.
